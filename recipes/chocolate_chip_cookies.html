Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Everyone loves a good chocolate chip cookie. And no one loved a chocolate chip cookie more than my Grandma Sue. I knew I could always count on her for that warm and welcoming smell of baking treats when I went to visit

She passed away a few years ago and I thought I'd never get to taste those cookies again. However...I recently discovered the recipe crumpled up in one of her aprons last week. And now you too can enjoy the taste and smell of Grandma Sue's cookies

this is actually from the recesses of my memory so enjoy

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Brown Sugar
  • Regular Sugar
  • Chocolate Chips
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla

Steps

  1. Make sure your butter is softened. Put brown sugar and regular sugar in a mixer with the softened butter. Beat until super creamy. And I mean creamy.
  2. Add your eggs and vanilla to the mixer.
  3. Combine all of your dry ingredients in a separate bowl
  4. Turn on mixer and slowwwwwwly add the dry ingredients a cup at a time into the sugar/butter mixutre to combine. (Unless you are an absolute mad lad and enjoy flour all over your floor)
  5. Remove mixing bowl from the mixer and fold in the chocolate chips. They like to be handled gently.
  6. At this point you'll remember that you do indeed need heat to bake cookies so turn that oven on to 375F
  7. Make the cookie dough into whatever size balls you'd like and place on a baking sheet. It's your cookie. Do what you want.
  8. Bake until you want. Again, it's your cookie. Like it gooey gooey? 6 mins. Like it crunchy crunch? 10 mins. Like it just right? 8 mins
